## Week 12 — Visual Prototyping: Village Exit → Fruit Collection

### Weekly Summary
This week I completed the **visual draft (prototype)** for the first two narrative scenes:

1. **The girl leaving the village**  
2. **The restricted fruit-collection task**

These drafts define the atmosphere, color palette, spatial layout, and emotional tone of early gameplay.  
They will later be integrated into Unity and connected to the EEG visualization layer in TouchDesigner.
